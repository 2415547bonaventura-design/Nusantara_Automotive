Tradition Meets Extreme Performance.

Delman GT adalah heritage hypercar concept yang memadukan elemen kendaraan tradisional Indonesia dengan teknologi otomotif modern.

Design Philosophy

Tradition Meets Performance menghadirkan perpaduan antara elemen visual tradisional, struktur modern, dan karakter unik yang tidak dimiliki mobil lain.

  • Traditional vehicle-inspired silhouette
  • Modern performance structure
  • Unique cultural identity
Exterior Highlights
  • Heritage Delman Body
  • Modern Spoke Wheels
  • Open Engine Pod
Interior Experience
  • Classic brown leather
  • Traditional carved wood panels
  • Modern aluminium trim
  • Wood-accent steering wheel
  • Open cabin layout
Performance
  • 5.2L Naturally Aspirated V10
  • 610 HP
  • 0–100 km/h: 3.5 s
  • Top Speed: 280 km/h
Chassis & Structure
  • Aluminium frame
  • Carbon fiber body panels
  • Lightweight structural balance
Concept Purpose
  • Show cultural identity in automotive design
  • Explore tradition and modern technology
  • Showcase concept vehicle innovation
Specifications Summary
  • Engine: 5.2L Naturally Aspirated V10
  • Power: 610 HP
  • Drivetrain: Concept layout
